About me

I'm a Frontend Developer and Product Designer, currently serving as the acting CTO at Braiding Nairobi. I specialise in designing and building inclusive, consumer-facing products for early-stage startups and growth-focused tech companies. My experience spans global teams and markets, from Singapore to London to Nairobi, delivering scalable, user-centred solutions.

At Braiding Nairobi, Im leading the end-to-end redesign of the mobile app and website. I transitioned the product from mobile-only to a cross-platform Flutter web app, and established a reusable component library to ensure design and development consistency. Working closely with internal and external stakeholders, including the CEO and engineering partners, Ive implemented new features, streamlined services through API integrations, and enhanced the overall product experience for both stylists and customers.

My work blends design leadership, product strategy, and UX implementation. I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments that value system thinking, creative problem-solving, and continuous iteration.
