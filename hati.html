HATI

Ministry of Health - Document Reader


HATI Document reader is a learning management system for health workers, created in the time of Covid-19.

The user is able to create an account as you would with any learning management system, and pick a course they would like to follow. The lessons are designed to be short and concise for quick and easy access for the user. While the screens have been designed in desktop view, the real world approach would be to create this as a PWA that is easily accesible on both desktop and mobile.

The project prototypes were built using Figma and were presented to the Ministry of Health in preparation for a grant application.
