Discover your next hairstyle, hairstylist and haircare.

The
Problem

  • Moving to new countries/cities makes it hard to find specialists. who do African Hair
  • Gaining access expert Haircare advice is tricky.
  • Most people have a stylist/salon they always go to, but appointment booking is not automated even though the haircare needs are frequent.

Kinyozi is a digital Haircare Assistant, letting you automate and schedule your haircare needs, from booking an appointment with your Hairstylist, Discovering new Salons and Hairstylists and Styles, and Leveraging AI to get recommendations on keeping your hair healthy.

The
Solution

Business
Objectives

Places like Kenyatta market and Salon chains like Ashleys are home to stylists from all walks of life, their contribution generating up to hundreds and millions of dollars annually. Many of these workers are in the informal sector of which, only 28% consist of women. Kinyozi provides an opportunity for a range of new stylists to gain access to clients. Apps such as Braiding Nairobi addresses this problem however Kinyozi proposes an extention on that solution.
There are a range of opportunites for business that Kinyozi can leverage:

  • Revenue from commission on booking fees
  • Advertising opportunity and further exposure for Hair products such as Darling
  • Freemium model for clients to access premium features such as personalised AI Haircare assistant.
